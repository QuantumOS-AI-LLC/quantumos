It’s time for a modern, AI-powered CRM.

Today, traditional CRM systems are a big letdown. They take too long to implement, they're difficult for teams to adopt, and they fail to provide a complete view of customer data.

HubSpot Smart CRM is different. It powers all of HubSpot’s marketing, sales, and customer service software by unifying your data, teams, and tech stacks on one customer platform. And with AI tools already baked in, it’s built to revolutionize the way your scaling business creates personalized customer experiences at scale.

Unify Your Data, Teams, and Tech Stacks

Get unified insights from all your customer data.

Incomplete data can threaten every part of your business: Marketing struggles with segmentation, Sales misses opportunities to engage with prospects, and Service lacks full visibility into customer issues.

But when all your customer data is connected through HubSpot Smart CRM, your teams get a shared, insightful, and complete view of every customer. This allows them to engage your customers in the right way, at the right time, and in the right place.

Popular Features

  • Custom Properties

    Associate specific data points with contacts, companies, and deals.

  • Duplicate Management

    Use artificial intelligence to identify and merge duplicate records.

  • Custom Objects

    Store almost any type of data in your CRM, and use it for marketing automation, better segmentation, and more personalized content.

  • Required Fields

    Define fields that must be completed by your team when certain actions are taken. Get more consistent data and more predictable outcomes.

  • Standard Contact Scoring

    Create rules-based scoring criteria. Focus your outreach based on any HubSpot data point, and use your scores to segment lists, trigger automation, and more.

  • Association Labels

    Create custom association labels for different pairs of standard CRM objects to specify the relationship between those objects.

HubSpot’s customer platform is made up of several premium products to help you engage with customers: Marketing Hub, Sales Hub, Service Hub, Content Hub, Operations Hub, and Commerce Hub.

Each product in the platform has a built-in connection to HubSpot Smart CRM, a foundational database that provides you with incredible insight into every contact at each stage in their customer journey. Although our products are powerful on their own, the real magic happens when you use them together.

HubSpot Smart CRM is automatically included with the purchase of any premium HubSpot software product. If you’d like to give more members of your team access to Smart CRM features, you can purchase additional seats.

You can also start using the free tools available in HubSpot Smart CRM and upgrade to one of our premium products for additional Smart CRM functionality when you’re ready.

Breeze Copilot and Breeze Agents, which are part of HubSpot’s collection of AI tools called Breeze, are optional features included throughout HubSpot’s Smart CRM depending on the product and edition you’re subscribed to. These AI tools enable you to create more engaging and personalized customer experiences, provide actionable insights, and help your teams streamline daily tasks. Learn more about Breeze.

In addition, HubSpot’s Breeze Intelligence tools can be purchased to improve your customer data even further by enriching contacts and companies in your Smart CRM database with third-party data from over 200 million buyer and company profiles. See the pricing page for details.
